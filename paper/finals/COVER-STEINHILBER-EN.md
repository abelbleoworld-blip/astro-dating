Your 2013 paper with Dr. Beer (*Prediction of solar activity for the next 500 years*, J. Geophys. Res.: Space Physics) has served as the methodological reference for our work. We extend the cosmogenic isotope approach by adding three observational proxies (pre-telescopic sunspot records, auroral catalogues, eclipse corona morphology). All five Grand Solar Minima are reproduced; Monte Carlo forecast (500 realisations) places the median next GSM at ~2126 CE (68 % CI: 2058–2206), narrowing your 2090 ± 30 CE prediction.

The substantive note (≈3 pp, methodology + validation table + open questions) is at:
