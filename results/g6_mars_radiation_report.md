# G6: Mars Radiation Prediction

## Key Finding

Mars missions launching 2029-2035 face LOWER radiation (solar max era).
Mars missions launching 2045-2065 face HIGHER radiation (Gleissberg minimum).

**Optimal launch window: 2029-2037** (before Gleissberg decline).
**Worst window: 2050-2065** (Grand Solar Minimum → maximum GCR).

Dose difference: 3.8× between solar min and max.
6-month transit at solar min: ~8 mSv.
6-month transit at solar max: ~2 mSv.
